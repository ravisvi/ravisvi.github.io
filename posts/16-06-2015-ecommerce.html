Curious case of Indian E-Commerce

#KyunKiya

Posted by Ravi Tejasvi on June 16, 2015 Picture source: wikipedia.org

Why do you lie to me. :’( How could you?

You would have heard of it. Great discounts, mega offers, best deals. Uno palabra. BULLSHIT. While there might be some genuine offers, but they are only a handful in a pool of fraudulent gambits. Hell, what is this guy saying? Let’s first explore why flipkart irks us junta and then look into the flamboyant cameo by our new player in the market, Shrimaan Paytm. (I’m not even going to talk about the arcane looking GUI of amazon shopping app. For one, it’s not about UI, and amazon isn’t swadeshi.)

Let’s talk about Flip(’sMeOff)kart.

Situation 1

Certain date xyz
Product? Seagate Hard Disk
Discounts? None.
Cost? INR 4k.

Certain date xyz+5 months.
Product? Seagate Hard Disk
Discounts? Yes. Hell yeah! 12% discount!
Cost?!? INR 4.2k.
Woooooow. Such Logic. Why? How can you even feel like doing this to your own company? Don’t you feel disgusted?
Well, one of the reasons may be that the price really is set by the seller and it’s not possible for flipkart to fix it. One thing. Automatic curation? If a product is uploaded, store it’s price. Next time another dealer puts it up for a higher price, and offers discount to reach an amount which is close to the average price of the product over a sample selling price of many dealers, get your representatives in touch with the dealer, don’t list the product! Why can’t this be done? It’s hard? There are better things to do? Come on! If you put genuine discount rates and even if they aren’t that great, customers would love you. Cause you provide them at our doorsteps. You make things easier. We know when we see a discount, that it truly, is a discount. :)

Situation 2

I order one item which costs 449 and the delivery charge is INR 100. Fair enough. However I need four of these. What do I do?
I increase the quantity to four right? These guys still charge, INR 100 for each product. Well. What? I’m ordering all of them at once. One delivery guy will come and deliver it. What are you guys doing?
If they indeed are getting me the four things on four different bikes, all coming in a formation whilst doing crazy acrobatics. I'd pay for that.





Are they charging that amount for the packaging of each thing? That seems plausible. But they have pooled it under delivery charges. Bothersome. I don’t like it.
( source : giphy.com )
Why are they doing this? I don’t know. They have enough resources and financial backing to make sure semantic errors like these does’t occur. But if they aren’t semantic errors, and there are actually costs involved, why not put a small info popup hovering additional delivery charges?

Flippy's Fiesty Cousin

Coming to flippy's feisty cousin paytm. I’m just gonna upload this.


( credits : Akhilesh San ) Erm. Look at the prices, enough ogling at the gorgeous model.

These things just leave me wondering as to why? Why are they doing this.
Quoting extreme prices and then tagging abnormal discounts to it might initially attract customers, capricious ones only. But in the long run, having the trust factor is important I guess. A little more work to curate what a dealer uploads can stop this disheartening trend. If there is no discount, say it. It’ll be much more appreciated. Am I being quixotic? I don’t think so.
Hope these amazing companies do something about this. If customer really comes first for you, then be honest. :)
