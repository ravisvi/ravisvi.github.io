Motivation to join Vmware

As I'm interested in building infrastructure that can rapidly analyse big data, VMware, an industry leader in virtual infrastrucure, was the natural choice. I got offered a role of MTS in the Storage Management team.

Daily work

I was part of designing and implementing VMware neXt generation VASA Provider (xVP) frontend.
Main goal of xVP is create performant and scalable VP implementation to reduce VMware's dependency on partner implementations.

Have implemented powercli commandlets for vSAN apis. Owing to huge demand in the market for VMware's powercli commandlets, we provided crucial and time saving vSAN commandlets for the 2016 release.
I also strive to drive innovation, by analyzing competitors, keeping a watch on the software trends related to storage and generate impactful ideas which can be turned into viable products/ features.
I also take part in performing code reviews and fixing product bugs.

Innovation

My team and I have demostrated a solution that decentralizes storage policy based management service from vCenter and converts it to a distributed service running on every ESXi host.
With this solution, each distributed host had copies of storage policies. Hence policies were no longer lost when vCenter was backed up. This also decreased the number of calls to the vCenter by 40% from each host hence improving the performance of the service.

Carrying VMware’s experience on cloud infrastructure, I’ve disclosed an invention about "Early Risk Detection System", bearing internal docket number D444.ID.
I’m also involved in VMware’s innovation series, Research and Development Innovation Offsite (RADIO). As part of this, I'm currently writing 2 research papers. One, focusing on improving performance and efficiency of distributed systems running VMware’s services on ESXi hosts by introducing a service which manages, load balances and schedules other services on the hosts; other, on predicting failures in a virtual infrastructure by analyzing the system logs. I have also collaborated and worked on container technologies and photon platform, trying to investigate container based distributed systems. For my efforts at VMware, I was one of the quickest to be promoted to MTS 2.
