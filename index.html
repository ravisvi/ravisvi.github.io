Hi!

I'm a software engineer in the Voice Asistant team at Google. My interests lie in the domains of reinforcement learning, neural networks and in general in applying Machine Learning techniques to build applications that are of high impact to customers. I find building prototypes with new technologies super exciting and try to do so at every opportunity I get.

I'm a computer science masters graduate from University of Massachusetts Amherst where I have completed some amazing courses like Reinforcement Learning by Dr. Phil Thomas, Neural Networks by Dr. Erik Learned-Miller, Machine Learning, Computer Vision and Algorithms & Systems for Data Science.

Before UMass, I was working in VMware RnD in Bangalore where I developed software that provided management suite for storage on vCenter.

Prior to VMware, I have interned as an IT Developer at Juniper Networks, co-founded a startup in the transport sector and have worked as student developer at Google Summer of Code for the open source organisation Freenet project.
